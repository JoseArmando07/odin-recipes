Tacos Recipe

Description:

Tacos is a very popular dish made in Mexico! Theyre delicious, and fast to make. they can be made with many different types of protein and tortilla.

For this recipe we will use ground beef as the protein.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Tomato Sauce & Water
  • Tortillas
  • Taco Toppings

How To Make:

  1. Cook the beef over a large skillet at medium high heat
  2. heat up the tortillas
  3. serve the meat in tortillas
  4. Add toppings of choice (guacamole,salsa,lettuce,cheese,etc...)
